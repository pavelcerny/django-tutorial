Welcome to Habit Tracker!

So you want to establish new habits and turn your life towards better? That’s awesome.
We at Habit Tracker want to help you to achieve your goals a bit by bit. Here’s how it works:

1. Add a new habit by pushing the red plus mark. We have some suggestions for you, but you can also create your own.
Give name to your habit and possibly a duration, like “Meditate for 10 minutes”, and how many times you want to do this per week.
Remember, it’s easier to start with small steps!

2. Every time you have accomplished one occurrence of the habit, you can mark it down on the habit bar. This way it’s easier for you to follow how you proceed!
Next to the bar is a small colorful bar that shows you how you are doing.

3. You can see statistics about your habits on the statistics page.
